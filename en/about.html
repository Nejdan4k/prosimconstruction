About Prosim Construction

Prosim Construction is a company in the Outaouais region specializing in renovation and post-disaster work. The company stands out for its rigor, quality of work and support offered to each client, from the first call until the end of the project.

Our team handles residential, commercial and industrial projects. We coordinate all stages: demolition, reconstruction, interior finishing and post-disaster cleanup.

Our mission is to restore your spaces quickly, while respecting standards, deadlines and the peace of mind of occupants.

Services for insurers

  • Post-disaster reconstruction and renovation
  • Barricading and site protection
  • Demolition
  • Decontamination and cleanup
  • 24/7 Emergency service
  • Cause investigation

Service area: Gatineau and surrounding areas (75 km radius).
